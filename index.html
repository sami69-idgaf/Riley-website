About

Owner of Elevate SD.

Hi! I'm Riley, owner of Elevate SD Mobile Detail. I'm a San Diego native, born and raised in Poway, and I've been detailing cars since I was 14 years old. After more than 20 years in retail management, I decided it was time to pursue my true passion — cars. I've always loved everything about them, especially the transformation that happens when a vehicle is brought back to life. There's nothing better than seeing a car look the way it's meant to.

When I started this business, my dad gave me simple but powerful advice: "Take care of your customers and do right by them, and they will always come back." I've carried that with me every day, along with my mom's words growing up: "Do it right, or don't do it at all." At Elevate SD, those values are at the core of everything we do — we are here to do it right, the first time.

We're a small, owner-operated mobile detailing business based in San Diego. That means I'm the one showing up, I'm the one doing the work, and I'm standing behind every job. No subcontractors, no shortcuts, no surprises on the invoice.
